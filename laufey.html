Who is she?

Laufey (Laufey Lín Bīng Jónsdóttir) is an Icelandic and Chinese classical musician and singer-songwriter. Having performed as a cello soloist with the Iceland Symphony Orchestra at age 15, Laufey was a finalist in the 2014 Ísland Got Talent. Soon after, she released her debut EP, Typical of Me, followed by Everything I Know About Love in 2022 and Bewitched in 2023. She rose to prominence for her success as a jazz-inspired pop artist.
