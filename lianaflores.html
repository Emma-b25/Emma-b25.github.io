Who is she?

Liana Flores is a Brazilian/British singer-songwriter and guitarist whose songs are characterized by calming vocals, acoustic guitar, and bossa nova and folk music inspiration. So far, she has released two extended plays, The Water's Fine! in 2018 and recently in 2019, as well as a debut studio album called Flower of the Soul.
