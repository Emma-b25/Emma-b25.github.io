Who is he?

Cavetown (Robin Skinner) is an English sing-songwriter, record producer, and Youtuber. His style is a mix of indie rock, indie pop, and bedroom pop with acoustic guitar and ukulele. So far, he has released five albums, the most recent being Worm Food. As a trans musician, their music has created a safe space for many young trans people around the world.
